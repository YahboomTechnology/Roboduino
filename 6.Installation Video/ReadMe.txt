Before installing the servo:
1. You need to download the code for the servo calibration to the Uno board.
2. Connect the servoto the expansion board.
The brown wire of each servo is connected to the black pin (GND) of the servo port (S1/S2/S3/S4) on the expansion board.
The red wire of each servo is connected to the red pin (VCC) of the servo port on the expansion board.
The orange wire of each servo is connected to the yellow pin (IO) of the servo port on the expansion board.
3. Turn on the power switch of the car.
4. Wait for the servo to rotate to 90бу.
5. Install the servo.
